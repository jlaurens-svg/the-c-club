Our philosophy

Travel without guilt.
Find them thriving.

At The C Club, your dog isn't "kept" — it's welcomed. We believe even the most cherished dog needs its own kind — a pack, space, play and landmarks.

Our days are designed like a dog's dream life : runs in the park, enrichment workshops, quiet moments in the shade of the olive trees, and humans who know them by name. You entrust a member of your family — we treat them like our own.

The estate

Between sea and hills,
a wooded haven.

Perched in the hills of Bordighera, minutes from the border and the French Riviera, our estate opens onto an enclosed park planted with olive, pine and palm trees.
